📊 Business Insights — Restaurant Sales Analysis
✅ 1. Revenue Performance Insight

The restaurant generated strong revenue primarily from a limited number of high-performing food items. A small group of menu items contributes to the majority of total sales revenue, indicating dependency on popular dishes.

Business Meaning:
Focusing on top-performing items can significantly increase profitability.

✅ 2. Product Performance Insight

Certain food items recorded the highest sales volume and revenue, showing strong customer preference toward specific menu categories.

Business Meaning:
These items should always remain well-stocked and prominently displayed on menus.

✅ 3. Category Analysis Insight

Main course categories generated higher revenue compared to starters and desserts, indicating that customers primarily visit for full meals rather than add-ons.

Business Meaning:
Restaurants can introduce combo meals including starters or desserts to increase average order value.

✅ 4. Customer Behavior Insight

A small number of customers contributed significantly higher spending compared to others, indicating the presence of loyal or repeat customers.

Business Meaning:
Loyalty programs and personalized offers can improve customer retention.

✅ 5. Payment Preference Insight

Digital payment methods were used more frequently than cash transactions, showing customer preference toward convenient and faster payment options.

Business Meaning:
Restaurants should optimize digital payment systems and provide cashback or online payment discounts.

✅ 6. Time-Based Sales Insight

Sales volume was higher on specific days of the week, indicating peak operational periods.

Business Meaning:
Staff allocation and inventory planning should be optimized during peak days to avoid service delays.

✅ 7. Order Value Insight

The average order value indicates moderate customer spending per visit, suggesting opportunities for upselling and cross-selling strategies.

Business Meaning:
Combo offers and meal upgrades can increase per-customer revenue.

✅ 8. Operational Insight

Sales trends reveal predictable demand patterns across months and weekdays, enabling better forecasting and inventory management.

Business Meaning:
Data-driven demand forecasting can reduce food wastage and operational costs.

💼 Business Recommendations 

Increase inventory for top-selling food items

Promote low-performing menu items through discounts

Introduce combo meal offers

Implement customer loyalty programs

Optimize staffing during peak sales periods

Encourage digital payments with incentives
